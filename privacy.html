Privacy Policy

KB Consulting LLC

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy describes how KB Consulting LLC ("KB Consulting," "we," "us") collects and uses information when you visit kb-impact.com.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly through the contact form or discovery call booking flow on this site. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your organization's name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number, if provided
  • Any message or scheduling details you include

We do not collect this information automatically. We only receive it when you submit the form or book a call.

How we use your information

We use the information you submit to respond to your inquiry and determine whether KB Consulting is a good fit for your organization. We do not use it for marketing, we do not sell it, and we do not share it with third parties except as described below.

Third-party services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts loads typography from Google's servers. Google may collect data about font requests in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • Formspree processes contact form submissions. Formspree transmits your submission to KB Consulting and stores it temporarily. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io.
  • Microsoft Bookings processes discovery call scheduling requests. Bookings are delivered to KB Consulting's Outlook calendar and stored there. Microsoft's privacy policy is available at microsoft.com.
  • GitHub Pages hosts this site. GitHub may log standard access information such as IP address and request time. GitHub's privacy policy is available at github.com.

We do not use advertising, analytics tracking, or cookies on this site.

Data retention

We retain contact form submissions and booking records only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry or to maintain records of our communications. If you would like your information removed, contact us at katie@kb-impact.com and we will delete it.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by emailing katie@kb-impact.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of this site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to katie@kb-impact.com.

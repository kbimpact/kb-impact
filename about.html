About

Katie Bartelt

Development Strategy and Donor Relations Consultant

For the past decade, I have built fundraising infrastructure for small and mid-size nonprofits in Sheboygan County and across Wisconsin. The work concentrates in youth services, community recreation, agriculture, and equine sectors.

KB Consulting operates as an embedded strategic advisor to small and mid-size nonprofits. The practice covers development assessment, fundraising strategy, donor data systems, board engagement, capital campaign readiness, and fundraising event strategy. Engagements are scoped with flat fees and named deliverables. The work produces infrastructure you can operate after I'm gone, not memos that sit on a shelf.

The practice concentrates in Sheboygan County and serves nonprofits across Wisconsin and the broader Midwest. Local community knowledge shapes the strategy. National frameworks do not account for which local foundations are stretched, which donors are already at the table for three other organizations, or which board networks have what relationships.
